Click, type, and enter with a . to send out a sentence as a message, and also place the sentence into the common document. Click, type, and enter with a ! to send out a remark upon an incoming sentence, or to dispel another's remark on one of your sentences. Remarking on a sentence will underline it before it is sent out, marking up the sentence and becoming part of a message thread. Dispelling a remark adds to the thread, and replaces the remark in the document. Click, type, and enter with a ? to ask a question. It will introduce a new topic for threads, as well as a new section of the document. It is possible that any question can be given a counterpart noun phrase suitable for draft section headings for the paragraphs that will contain its answer (e.g. "Where is Paris?" could be translated into "Location of Paris").
